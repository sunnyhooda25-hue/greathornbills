SaaS Marketing Workflow Cheatsheet
Reduce CAC • Scale Growth • Optimize Campaigns • 2026 Edition
📊 Marketing Fundamentals
Key Metrics:
CAC = Spend / New Customers
LTV:CAC Ratio Target: 3:1
Health Check:
  • CAC trending down
  • MQL→SQL >15%
  • Pipeline <45 days
  • Churn <5%/month
🎯 3-Tier Funnel
TOFU (Awareness)
  • SEO, Content, Social
  • Metrics: Traffic, Engagement
MOFU (Consideration)
  • Email, Webinars, Retargeting
  • Metrics: MQLs, Downloads
BOFU (Conversion)
  • Paid Search, Sales
  • Metrics: SQLs, Revenue
💰 Cut CAC 30-40%
Week 1-2: Audit
  • Identify wasted spend
  • Map full attribution
  • Find leaky funnel stages
Week 3-6: Optimize
  • Pause ROAS <2
  • Double down winners
  • Improve LP CVR
Week 7-12: Scale
  • Expand winning campaigns
  • Automate what works
🚀 Google Ads
DO:
  • Exact match keywords
  • Competitor terms
  • A/B test bi-weekly
  • Aggressive negatives
DON'T:
  • Broad match expensive KW
  • Traffic to homepage
  • Set and forget
  • Skip conversion tracking

Analytics:

Automation:

Attribution:

📅 Daily Workflow

  • Optimize campaigns
  • A/B test review
EOD:
  • Update dashboards
  • Note learnings
📈 Attribution
Models:
  • First-Touch
  • Last-Touch
  • Linear (equal credit)
  • Time Decay
  • U-Shaped (40/20/40)
Best: U-shaped
40% first, 40% last, 20% middle
