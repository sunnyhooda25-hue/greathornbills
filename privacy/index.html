Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 14, 2026

Introduction

Great Hornbills ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal data of our users and prospects. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share information when you interact with our website (greathornbills.com), our advertising on Facebook and Instagram, and our services.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Lead Form Submissions via Meta (Facebook & Instagram): When you submit a lead form through our ads on Facebook or Instagram, we collect your full name, email address, phone number, business details, website URL, monthly revenue range, project timeline, and any other information you voluntarily provide in our lead forms.
  • Website Contact Forms: When you submit a contact form on greathornbills.com, we collect your name, email, phone number, and message content.
  • Booking & Appointment Data: When you book a call via Calendly or similar scheduling tools, we collect your name, email, selected time slot, and any notes you provide.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with our website and ads, including pages visited, links clicked, time spent, and referral sources through Meta Pixel, Google Analytics, and similar tools.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser type, IP address, and general location (city/country level).

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your inquiries and provide requested services
  • Send you marketing strategy briefs, quotes, and proposals
  • Schedule consultation calls and follow up on leads
  • Improve our services, website, and advertising performance
  • Measure advertising effectiveness through Meta Pixel, Conversions API, and Google Analytics
  • Create lookalike audiences for future advertising (in aggregated, anonymized form)
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect our legal rights

Data Sharing and Third-Party Service Providers

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only with trusted service providers who help us operate our business, including:

  • Meta Platforms (Facebook & Instagram) — for lead collection and advertising
  • Google — for analytics and search performance tracking
  • Email service providers — for automated follow-up emails
  • Calendly or similar booking tools — for scheduling calls
  • Zapier and n8n — for workflow automation between tools
  • Google Sheets / CRM tools — for lead management
  • Cloud hosting and storage providers

All service providers are bound by confidentiality obligations and use your data only for the purposes we specify.

International Data Transfer

Great Hornbills is based in India and operates globally. When you submit information, your data may be transferred to, stored, and processed in India, the United States, or other countries where our service providers operate. We take reasonable steps to ensure your data receives an adequate level of protection regardless of where it is processed.

Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, typically up to 36 months for lead data, or as required by applicable laws. You may request earlier deletion at any time.

Your Rights (GDPR, CCPA & Other Privacy Laws)

Depending on your location, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request deletion of your personal data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing at any time
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Receive a copy of your data in a portable format
  • Opt out of the sale of personal information (CCPA)
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

To exercise any of these rights, please email us at hello@greathornbills.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your data at any time by emailing hello@greathornbills.com with the subject line "Data Deletion Request." We will process your request and confirm deletion within 30 days.

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website and advertising use cookies and similar tracking technologies including Meta Pixel, Google Tag Manager, Google Analytics, and Meta Conversions API to measure advertising performance, optimize user experience, and retarget visitors. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings or our cookie banner.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe we have collected data from a minor, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top.
