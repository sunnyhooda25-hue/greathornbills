Common questions

Before your first visit

Do you accept my insurance?
We accept Blue Cross Blue Shield, Aetna, Harvard Pilgrim, Cigna, and United. If you don't see yours listed, reach out — we may still be in-network or able to provide a superbill for out-of-network reimbursement.
Is telehealth available?
Yes — we offer secure, HIPAA-compliant video visits to anyone located in Massachusetts. Most patients choose a mix of in-person and telehealth appointments.
How long until I can be seen?
We typically offer free 15-minute consultations within 3-5 business days and full intake appointments within 1-2 weeks.
What conditions do you treat?
We specialize in adult mental health including anxiety disorders, depression, ADHD, trauma/PTSD, bipolar disorder, and life transitions. If you're unsure whether we're the right fit, book a free consult — we'll help you figure it out.
Do you prescribe controlled medications?
Yes, when clinically appropriate. We follow all Massachusetts regulations and prefer a thoughtful, collaborative approach to prescribing.
