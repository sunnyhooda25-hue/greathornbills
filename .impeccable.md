## Design Context

### Users
- **Primary:** Founders, marketing heads, and decision-makers at SMBs/startups looking for a digital marketing agency
- **Context:** Arriving from paid ads (Google, Meta, LinkedIn) on mobile devices. They've seen an ad promising results and are evaluating whether to trust this agency with their budget
- **Job to be done:** Quickly assess credibility → understand services → take action (contact or book call)
- **Time on page:** 30-90 seconds typical for paid traffic

### Brand Personality
- **Voice:** Bold, direct, results-obsessed
- **Tone:** Confident without being arrogant. Marketing-first, tech-second.
- **3 words:** Bold. Results-Driven. No-BS.
- **Emotional goal:** "These people have been in the trenches. They know what works."

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Visual tone:** Dark, premium, modern — inspired by Healthy Together's Webflow design
- **Primary colors:** Navy (#101722), Electric blue (#4541fe), Cream (#f5f3ff)
- **Accent:** Pink/magenta gradient (hero waveform), Neon green (data accents)
- **Theme:** Dark mode primary, cream/lavender for contrast sections
- **Typography:** Bold sans-serif headings (Impact/compressed feel), clean body text
- **Anti-references:** Generic agency sites with stock photos, template-looking sites, overly corporate

### Design Principles
1. **Revenue over aesthetics** — Every section must earn its place. If it doesn't build trust or drive action, cut it.
2. **Mobile-first for conversions** — Paid traffic is 70%+ mobile. Every pixel must serve the thumb-scrolling visitor.
3. **Show, don't tell** — Real metrics, real client logos, real testimonials. No vague promises.
4. **Speed is trust** — Fast load = professional. Every millisecond of load time costs conversions.
5. **Two paths to conversion** — Always offer both "Get in Touch" (form) and "Book a Call" (Calendly). Different buyers prefer different actions.

### Conversion Priorities
1. Sticky CTA bar (always visible on mobile)
2. Contact form popup (Get in Touch)
3. Calendly booking (Book a Call)
4. Social proof (client logos, testimonials, metrics)
5. Service overview (what we do)

### Tech Stack
- Single-file HTML (index.html) with inline CSS/JS
- Webflow CSS framework (webflow.min.css)
- GSAP + ScrollTrigger for animations
- Lenis for smooth scroll
- Text-split for word-by-word heading animations
- GitHub Pages hosting, Cloudflare CDN
