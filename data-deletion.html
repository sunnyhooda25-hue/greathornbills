User Data Deletion Instructions

How to Request Deletion of Your Data

Great Hornbills respects your right to control your personal data. If you wish to delete the data we have collected through your use of our application, you can follow the steps below.

Option 1: Delete via Facebook Settings

  1. Go to your Facebook Settings & Privacy → Settings
  2. Click on Apps and Websites
  3. Find Great Hornbills (or the app name) and click Remove
  4. Check the box that says "Delete all posts, photos, and videos on Facebook that the app posted on your behalf"
  5. Click Remove to confirm

Option 2: Contact Us Directly

  1. Send an email to info@greathornbills.com
  2. Use the subject line: "Data Deletion Request"
  3. Include your full name and the email address associated with your account
  4. We will process your request and delete all your data within 30 days
  5. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete

What Data Gets Deleted

Upon receiving your deletion request, we will permanently remove all personal data associated with your account, including your name, email address, profile information, and any usage data collected through our application.

Data Retention After Deletion

Once we process your deletion request, your data will be permanently removed from our active systems within 30 days. Some data may be retained in encrypted backup systems for up to 90 days before being permanently purged.
