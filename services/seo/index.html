Frequently Asked Questions

What is SEO?
SEO (Search Engine Optimization) is the practice of optimizing your website to rank higher in search engine results pages (SERPs). This includes technical optimization, content creation, on-page optimization, and building backlinks to increase organic visibility and traffic.
How long does SEO take to show results?
SEO typically takes 3-6 months to show meaningful results, though some improvements can be seen sooner. Competitive keywords may take 6-12 months. SEO is a long-term investment that compounds over time, with results building month after month.
How much does SEO cost?
SEO services typically range from $1,000-5,000/month for small to medium businesses. Enterprise SEO can cost $5,000-20,000+/month. Cost depends on competition in your industry, current website state, and growth goals.
Is SEO better than paid ads?
SEO and paid ads serve different purposes. SEO builds long-term organic traffic that doesn't stop when you stop paying. Paid ads provide immediate results but stop when budget runs out. Most successful businesses use both strategically.
What's the difference between on-page and off-page SEO?
On-page SEO refers to optimizations on your website: content, meta tags, internal linking, page speed. Off-page SEO refers to external factors: backlinks, brand mentions, social signals. Both are essential for ranking well.
