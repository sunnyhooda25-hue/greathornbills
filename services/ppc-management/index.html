Frequently Asked Questions

What is PPC management?
PPC (Pay-Per-Click) management is the process of overseeing and optimizing paid advertising campaigns on platforms like Google Ads, LinkedIn, and Meta. It includes keyword research, ad creation, bid management, A/B testing, and performance optimization to maximize ROI.
How much does PPC management cost?
PPC management fees typically range from 10-20% of ad spend or $1,000-5,000/month for flat-fee retainers. This is separate from your actual ad spend budget. Most agencies require minimum ad spend of $3,000-5,000/month.
How long before PPC shows results?
PPC can generate leads and sales within days of launching. However, optimization takes 2-4 weeks as we gather data, test audiences, and refine campaigns. Most campaigns reach optimal performance within 60-90 days.
Google Ads vs Facebook Ads - which is better?
Google Ads captures high-intent searchers actively looking for solutions. Facebook/Meta Ads excels at demand generation and reaching users who aren't actively searching. Most businesses benefit from using both platforms strategically.
What's a good CPA for paid advertising?
A good CPA depends on your business model and margins. For e-commerce, aim for CPA below 20-30% of AOV. For B2B SaaS, CPA should be under 1/3 of first-year customer value. We benchmark against your unit economics.
