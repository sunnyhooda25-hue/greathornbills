Frequently Asked Questions

What is e-commerce marketing?
E-commerce marketing encompasses all strategies used to drive traffic, conversions, and revenue for online stores. This includes paid advertising (Google, Meta, TikTok), SEO, email marketing, influencer partnerships, and conversion rate optimization.
How much should I spend on e-commerce marketing?
Most e-commerce brands spend 10-30% of revenue on marketing. For new stores, start with $2,000-5,000/month in ad spend plus agency fees. Scale based on ROAS — aim for 3x+ ROAS before scaling aggressively.
What's a good ROAS for e-commerce?
A good ROAS depends on your margins. For most e-commerce brands, 3x-4x ROAS is profitable. High-margin products (50%+) can be profitable at 2x ROAS. Low-margin products may need 5x+ ROAS.
Which platforms work best for e-commerce advertising?
Meta (Facebook/Instagram) works best for discovery and impulse purchases. Google Shopping and Search work best for high-intent buyers. TikTok is growing rapidly for younger demographics.
How long until I see results?
Paid ads can generate sales within days, but optimization takes 2-4 weeks. Email marketing shows results immediately for existing customers. SEO takes 3-6 months. Allow 90 days to properly evaluate a marketing strategy.
